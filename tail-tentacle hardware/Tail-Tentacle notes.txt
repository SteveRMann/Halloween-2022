The original breadboard had a PINHD-1X8 for the input side, but the production version uses six pins.

Prototype pinout:
1 - Ground
2 - +5V
3 - Ground
4 - +5V
5 - 5V isolation if the ESP is to be powered from the USB
6 - +5V to the ESP
7 - External trigger button
8 - Ground

The production version:
